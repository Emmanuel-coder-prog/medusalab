# Wholesale Pricing Decision

Requirement:
Approved wholesale buyers receive special prices.

Chosen implementation:
Medusa Customer Groups + Price Lists.

Why:
No company lifecycle, credit account, buyer approvals, quotes,
or purchase orders are required in this phase.

Rejected:
A custom B2B module.

Trigger to revisit:
Create a B2B module only when company-level rules or a purchase workflow
becomes necessary.
